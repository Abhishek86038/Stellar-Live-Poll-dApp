# Demo Video Guide

To record the 1-minute demo video for this project:

1. Use a screen recorder (e.g. OBS Studio, Loom, QuickTime).
2. Start `npm start` to run the dApp.
3. Open your browser to `http://localhost:3000`.
4. Steps to show in the video:
   - Click "Connect Wallet"
   - Select Freighter in the modal.
   - Wait for the Loading Skeletons to disappear showing caching.
   - Wait 5 seconds to observe the "Last updated: X seconds ago" counter adjust.
   - Click a poll option and cast a vote.
   - Wait for the success Toast Notification to appear.
   - If connected as a creator, show the "Close Poll" button.
5. Save the video and upload it.
